## DidactaX = Teaching & knowledge creation, amplified.
## Designed to teach, instruct, and explain clearly.
DidactaX is an intelligent writing and learning platform built to support note-taking, book authoring, and educational content creation. It enables teachers to design lessons, organize curriculum materials, and guide learners, while allowing users to transform ideas into structured notes and complete written works. DidactaX focuses on clarity, pedagogy, and long-term knowledge reuse.
